Pipeline Stages
●
Working
Inquiry received, actively in conversation. Date offered or TBD. Not yet committed to booking.
●
Seal the Deal ⚡
Party date is imminent — within days. Client is warm or has expressed intent to pay. Needs immediate follow-up to convert.
●
Booked
Deposit paid and confirmed. Party is locked in on the calendar.
●
Did Not Book
Client stopped responding, went in a different direction, or friends cancelled. Closed without a booking.
●
⚠ At Risk
A Working inquiry with no response logged in 24+ hours. Lead is in danger of going cold.

🏠
Off-Site
Party is held at an external location (e.g. Air BnB). Mo must approve before confirming.
💰
Big Money
